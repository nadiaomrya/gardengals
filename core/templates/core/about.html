ABOUT GARDEN GALS OF GEORGIA

We're passionate about creating beautiful, sustainable gardens.

OUR STORY

Garden Gals of Georgia was officially founded in 2020 by Bailey Price, expert gardener and landscape designer inspired by a spirit of service to the community.

We love our clients as much as we love our work, and we believe in the power of nature to transform lives and heal the soul.

Our mission is to create beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces that bring joy and tranquility to our clients' lives.
